Where do we fly?

Welcome to Las Vegas Balloon Rides! We are a full service hot air balloon ride company serving the Las Vegas and surrounding areas. Las Vegas Balloon Rides is owned and operated by Doug Campbell, a FAA certified hot air balloon pilot. All pilots that fly at Las Vegas Balloon Rides have a perfect safety record and our number one goal is to keep it that way!

We offer different balloon rides:

The Standard Balloon flight with T breakfast after landing; This is the standard shared flight, usually 4 up to 6/8 people on board the balloon.
